Our Story

The IUS Quiz System was born out of a vision to revolutionize the way students learn and assess their knowledge. Recognizing the challenges faced by university students in Bangladesh, we set out to create a platform that would make learning more interactive, engaging, and accessible.

Founded in 2020, our platform began as a small project aimed at helping students in the International University of Scholars (IUS) prepare for their examinations. Over time, we've grown into a comprehensive learning management system serving students across multiple departments including Computer Science, Business Administration, Electrical Engineering, English, and Textile Engineering.

Today, our platform hosts thousands of quizzes covering a wide range of subjects and topics. We're committed to continuous improvement, regularly adding new features based on student feedback and the latest educational research. Our goal is not just to help students pass exams, but to foster a genuine love for learning and academic excellence.
